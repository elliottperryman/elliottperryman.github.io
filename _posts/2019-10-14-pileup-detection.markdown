I was an undergraduate student within the Nab Experiment, where I worked on Fourier filtering detector signals. I gave a talk at the 2019 DNP meeting. In this talk I used a simple convolutional neural network to identify pileup, which is when signals arrive very close in time such that they can be confused for a single event.
